Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started with ToolHub?

Simply visit our tools page and start using any of our free tools. No registration required for basic features. For advanced features like Google Analytics integration, you can sign up with your Google account.

Is my data secure when using ToolHub?

Yes, we take security seriously. Files are processed securely and automatically deleted within 24 hours. We don't store your files permanently, and all data transmission is encrypted.

What file formats do you support?

We support a wide range of file formats including PDF, DOC, DOCX, JPG, PNG, GIF, MP4, and many more. Each tool displays the supported formats on its respective page.

How do I connect Google Analytics?

Click "Sign in with Google" and grant permission to access your Analytics data. This allows our SEO tools to provide real insights about your website's performance.

What's the difference between free and pro plans?

Free users get access to basic tools with usage limits. Pro users enjoy unlimited usage, advanced SEO features, Google Analytics integration, and priority support.

Can I use ToolHub for commercial purposes?

Yes! All our plans, including the free tier, allow commercial use. For heavy commercial usage, we recommend upgrading to our Pro or Enterprise plans.

How do I report a bug or request a feature?

You can contact us through our contact page or email support@toolhub.com. We appreciate feedback and actively work on user-requested features.
